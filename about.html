Our Mission & History

Established in 2005, Montessori School & Childcare has been a trusted name in Austin's private school landscape. Our school has grown from a small group of dedicated teachers and families to a thriving community of over 200 students.

Our teaching approach is centered around the Montessori method, which emphasizes hands-on learning, individualized instruction, and peer-to-peer collaboration. Our experienced teachers foster a love of learning that extends beyond the classroom.

From music and art to sports and outdoor education, our extracurricular programs help students develop their passions and build a sense of community. We believe that a well-rounded education includes opportunities for character development and social growth.
