Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5, focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development through play-based learning and hands-on activities.

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from grades K-4, emphasizing a strong foundation in core subjects, as well as character development and community service.

📝

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper school program serves students from grades 5-8, providing a challenging and supportive environment for academic growth, leadership development, and extracurricular exploration.
